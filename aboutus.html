Our Story

Helping Hands Youth Poverty Emancipation Foundation was born out of a profound personal journey. Joseph Tobias, our founder, grew up amidst the challenging conditions of Northern Nigeria, where he endured hunger, hopelessness, and abandonment. Branded with the label of having 'no future ambition,' Joseph's path from despair to empowerment exemplifies the incredible strength of resilience and the transformative power of support. What helped Joseph overcome these challenges was a deep faith, the power of education, and the support of a community of friends and strangers through scholarships and other forms of assistance. These crucial elements provided him with the hope and resources needed to break free from the cycle of darkness caused by poverty. At Helping Hands, we are dedicated to offering the same kind of support to young Nigerians. We believe that with the right opportunities, resources, and community backing, every young person can overcome adversity and achieve their full potential. Our mission is to foster a future where hope and success are within reach for all, transforming lives and rewriting the narratives of countless young people.

ABOUT HELPINGHANDS

OUR VISION & MISSION

There are two parts to our Missions

 • A Nigeria where poverty is no longer a hindrance to the dreams and aspirations of any young person

• To empower young Nigerians to rise above poverty and to create a bright future through skill development, education, and mindset transformation.
